About DaRa

Singer

DaRa is an Irish singer. While he primarily makes relaxed, acoustic covers of modern poular songs, he also peforms songs from other genres such as rock, country, and pop.

Instrumentalist

The guitar is the predominant instrument that features in DaRa's music. However, he plays other instruments such as the ukulele, tin whistle, flute, piano, as well as some percussion instruments.

Composer

While primarily a cover artist, DaRa writes and performs his own songs. His music would best be described as folk-rock with elements of soul and country.

Performer

Although YouTube is his main focus, DaRa sometimes performs at various events, when requested. He has performed at events such as weddings, and local concerts and festivals, among others.
